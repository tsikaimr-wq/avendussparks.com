1. Minimum Withdrawal Amount

The minimum withdrawal amount is 100. Requests below this threshold will not be processed.

2. Withdrawal Time Window

Withdrawals can be submitted Monday to Friday (excluding public holidays) between 09:30 and 15:00. Requests made outside of this time window will be processed on the next business day.

3. Bank Information Verification

Users must ensure that all bank account information provided is accurate and up-to-date. The company is not responsible for any failed, delayed, or misdirected transfers resulting from incorrect or outdated bank details.

Withdrawal requests are typically processed within 24 to 48 hours after approval. The actual crediting time may vary depending on the policies and processing capabilities of your financial institution.

5. Bank Policies and Conditions

Please note the following conditions regarding bank withdrawals: Processing times and procedures may vary among financial institutions. Additional identity or compliance verification may be required before processing certain withdrawals.

6. Compliance and Review

All withdrawal requests are subject to compliance checks, anti-money laundering (AML) monitoring, and internal risk control procedures. Withdrawals found to be linked to suspicious activity may be temporarily withheld pending investigation.
